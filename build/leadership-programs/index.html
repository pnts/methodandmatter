What You Can Expect

Experiential learning immersions for leadership teams looking to increase their capacity to take on big challenges, be more creative, and build cultures of excellence.

Leadership programs are designed in collaboration with team leaders. We take the biggest challenges and opportunities facing your team, look for patterns in the data from surveys and other assessment tools, interview team members, and create a program experience custom designed to support you and your leadership teams.

I also run Leader as Coach in-house for organizations that want to support their leaders in developing a coaching leadership style. This includes the option to extend the engagement with peer coaching circles, a fantastic way for leaders to deepen their coaching practice and support their teams.
