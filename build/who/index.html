Who I Work With

I coach senior leaders, executives, and teams who sense that these times call for different ways of seeing and being in organizations. I partner with people who feel the tension between business-as-usual expectations and the deeper currents of change moving through our world, who are ready to develop new capacities for attention, awareness, and leadership.

You Believe...

  • that beneath metrics and OKRs lies an invitation to work differently
  • that organizational life can contribute to wider systemic flourishing
  • that wisdom emerges through deep listening and attunement
  • that slowing down creates the conditions for deeper awareness
  • that business can be regenerative rather than extractive
  • that organizations can be places of genuine transformation
  • that leadership includes tending to the systems we're part of

You are ready and willing to...

  • explore different ways of seeing and being in organizations
  • listen deeply to what is emerging in and around you
  • develop new capacities for attention and awareness
  • question familiar patterns and assumptions
  • see how you are implicated in larger systems
  • engage in practices of attunement
  • be accountable for your own transformation
  • stay curious in the face of complexity
  • find joy in the journey of discovery

You long for...

  • ways to navigate complexity with wisdom and care
  • authentic connection with your team and the wider world
  • clarity about your unique role in these transformative times
  • space to attend to what matters most
  • the capacity to work differently in times of great change
  • wisdom to lead through uncertainty and emergence

Who I don't work with

I'm likely not the right coach if you are...

  • seeking quick fixes or tactical solutions
  • wanting to maintain business-as-usual approaches
  • looking for a conventional leadership playbook
  • resistant to exploring different ways of seeing and being
  • unwilling to question familiar patterns and assumptions
  • expecting transformation without personal investment
  • solely focused on metrics and shareholder value
  • uncomfortable with uncertainty and emergence
