Availability

All meetings are scheduled with advanced notice. I do most of my scheduling through Calendly. Sessions may be canceled or rescheduled with at least 48 hours notice. Sessions canceled or rescheduled within 48 hours will be counted as complete.

I work with clients Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday from 11am - 6pm, either on Zoom or a phone call. For clients based in Europe and Africa, alternate time slots are available.

Timing and Cadence

I see most clients on a bi-weekly cadence for 60 minutes. Folks I've been working with for a while, where we have established a strong relationship of trust and a foundation of work together, may choose to meet ever three or four weeks. And sometimes, when the need is more urgent, I work with folks on a weekly basis.
