For senior leaders facing complex organizational challenges who want to do meaningful work without losing themselves.

Does it sometimes feel like you have to be someone you’re not in order to be an effective leader?

You’ve worked hard to get here. Some days you’re on fire—hitting your stride, showing up powerfully, communicating clearly, and seeing the impact of your work. Other days you leave meetings reactive and exhausted, wondering where your confidence went.

This push-pull between flow and frustration is common on the leadership journey. The good news? You don’t need another leadership model to squeeze yourself into. You need practices that help you show up with presence, build trust, and drive results—all while staying true to who you are.

METHOD & MATTER is a leadership and work coaching company based in San Francisco, offering executive coaching for leaders and teams, cohort-based leadership programs, and in-person experiential offsites.
