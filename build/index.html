Executive coaching for unconventional leaders.

Working at the intersection of business, design, leadership, and people, method + matter combines integral coaching with appreciative inquiry, design thinking, organziational design, and business strategy to help leaders flourish.

You don't have to go it alone.

Your leadership journey is unique, but it doesn't have to be lonely. If you are feeling stuck, challenged, or uncertain, working with a coach - a trusted partner and loyal ally - can open up new worlds of possibility, impact, and effectiveness.

Coaching

Playing off a foundation of Intentional Change Theory, Appreciative Inquiry, and Integral Coaching, method + matter takes a strengths-based approaches to change. Working with both individuals and teams, method + matter fosters authentic, unconventional leadership that builds better organizations and outcomes.
