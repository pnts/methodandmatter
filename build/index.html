For senior leaders facing complex organizational challenges who want to do meaningful work without losing themselves.

Does it sometimes feel like you have to be someone you’re not in order to be an effective leader?

You’ve worked hard to get here. Some days you’re on fire, and other days you feel reactive and exhausted, wondering where your confidence went.

This oscillation between flow and frustration is common on the leadership journey, and frequently shows up when leaders are pushing up against their growth edge, where fear based patterns and limiting beliefs can be the loudest.

The good news is that there is nothing wrong with you. This is, in fact, a wonderful place to be. It is a sign that you are ready to evolve your leadership into something more aligned and true to who you really are.

METHOD & MATTER is a leadership and work coaching company based in San Francisco, offering executive coaching for leaders and teams, cohort-based leadership programs, and in-person experiential offsites.
