The method + matter approach

My approach is built on a foundation of Integral Coaching. The purpose of this style of coaching is to use a person's entire world as material for developing self-generating, self-sustaining practices that will be relevant through the arc of their career and life. Integral Coaching is not coaching for compliance, or coaching for accountability. It is deeply humanistic work that opens up insights and possibilities for new paths forward.

Client programs are designed around developmental outcomes, where success is measured by a change in observable behaviors. Over the course of 6 months (12 sessions), I work with people to deepen their understanding of what has them stuck at this point in their lives, and to unwind unhelpful patterns of behavior that act to keep the stuckness in place.

Coaching is a co-creative process, but structurally a typical program includes:

  • A 90 minute intake session where I work to understand where you are at, what is coming up for you, and what your understanding of your current situation is
  • 12 individual coaching sessions over the course of 6 months
  • A personalized coaching program designed with developmental outcomes for your context and goals
  • Tailored practices to support new ways of leading, working, and showing up in the world
  • Resources for continued learning and development
  • Recordings of each session and follow-up notes
  • Email and text support in between sessions (I'm here for you!)

While it is understood that we are all busy with careers, family, friends, hobbies, and life as it generally unfolds, this type of work does require committment and time set aside each day to engage in practices, reflections, and exercises to facilitate the work of transformation and change. Depending on the person, this can be anywhere from 15 minutes a day to an hour or more. Don't panic! I can work with what you have going on to find a suitable program that works for you.
