Shaping leadership and life.

Increase the coherence between vocation and values, embody presence in the midst of uncertainty, and take creative, choiceful action.

Sometimes it can feel like everything has gone out of alignment. That inexplicably, and maybe quite suddenly, what worked before is no longer working. When this happens we have two options - ignore it and keep pushing forward as usual, or listen and evolve.

method & matter is a resource for leaders and seekers who feel called to alchemize these unique moments, transforming what no longer serves into fuel for deep and lasting change.

Hello! I'm Andi.

I'm an executive coach working at the intersection of leadership, business, psychology, and design.

I believe that work is an invitation to both professional and personal growth, and that the challenges and opportunities we encounter each day can be transformational catalysts for individuals, teams, organizations, and society.

I partner with leaders to build effective organizations, high-performing teams, and lasting change.

Kitchen Party: A Newsletter for Leaders and Seekers

Whenever there is a house party people inevitably gather in the kitchen, convivially packed together with warmth and openness.

The environment encourages the sharing of ideas, possibilities, and dreams, subtly connecting and altering all who are present. Kitchen Party is a newsletter built with this ethos in mind, inviting explorations at the intersection of leadership, life, and what it means to be human.
