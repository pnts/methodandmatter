Hi, I'm Andrea

I founded method & matter because I believe in leaders like you. Leaders that don't necessarily "look" like the dominant leadership archetype. My personal leadership journey was fraught with doubt, derision, and failures as I struggled to find a way of leading that was aligned with my values and vision. With the guidance of an executive coach, I began a journey of unwinding beliefs that no longer served me, while cultivating a connection with my power, purpose, and leadership embodiment.

The work was nothing less than transformational. And I want this for you, too.

Before becoming a coach I lead product and design teams for startups in the B2B SaaS space, and before that I taught business English in Japan for four years. I'm currently the Vice President of the Interaction Design Association, have an MBA from the Weatherhead School of Management, a BA in Technocultural Studies from Oberlin College, and am a certified Integral Coach through New Ventures West.
