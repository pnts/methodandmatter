Hi, I'm Andrea.

You can call me Andi.

I founded method & matter because I believe in leaders like you. Leaders that don't necessarily "look" like the dominant leadership archetype. My personal leadership journey was fraught with doubt, derision, and failures as I struggled to find a way of leading that was aligned with my values and vision. With the guidance of an executive coach, I began a journey of unwinding beliefs that no longer served me, while cultivating a connection with my power, purpose, and embodied leadership.

The work was nothing less than transformational. And I want this for you, too.

Before becoming a coach I lead product and design teams for startups in the B2B SaaS space, and before that I taught business English in Japan for four years. I'm currently the President of the Interaction Design Association, have an MBA from the Weatherhead School of Management, a BA in Technocultural Studies from Oberlin College, and am certified through New Ventures West, The International Coaching Federation, and the Enneagram Institute.

Land Acknowledgement: I reside on the unceded ancestral homeland of the Ramaytush Ohlone peoples who are the original inhabitants of the San Francisco Peninsula. With deepest respect to the Ancestors, Elders and Relatives of the Ramaytush Community, I affirm their sovereign rights as First Peoples. I am committed to dismantling ongoing legacies of settler colonialism and white supremacy through study, service, donations, Yunakin Land Tax, and my own inner-work.
